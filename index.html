Magic Cat in a Purple Hat

Magic Cat in a Purple Hat is a single player strategy card game where you play as Smudge the cat on his journey to become the best street magician.

Magic Cat in a Purple Hat was developed during the 2019 schoool year. Working along side six other people on a team by the name Purple Hat Productions, our team develpoed and presented Magic Cat in a Purple Hat as our MI 497 project, Game Design Studdio, at Michigan State University.

I served as Lead Sound Designer and backup Gameplay Programmer.

As Lead Sound Designer, I was in charge of the following:

  • Self-taught myself Music Production tools such as Logic Pro X and Roli.
  • Produced all the music/character themes, along with SFX to bring juice to the game.
  • Implemented an Audio Manager to organize all sounds in one place for easy integration. This allowed for music to be called at the appropriate times without causing a mesh of sounds.

    • As a Gameplay Programmer, I helped the Lead Programmer with the following:

  • Reviewed and cleaned any zombie code for readable code documentation.
  • Helped with calling sound effects and theme music at the right moments for appropriate feeback for the player.
